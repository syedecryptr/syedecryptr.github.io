A Two way web Applet for calculating DFT and IDFT

DFT

Enter the sequence of x[n] with One space in between two different elements of sequence.
**Dont give space in the end and beginning**
✔ 34 54 23 65 76 23 90
✘ 2, 4, 7, 34, 7, 23
✘ 3,6,10,15,54,1,3

IDFT

Enter the sequence of x[n] with One space in between two different elements of sequence.
**Dont give space in the end and beginning**
✔ 34+3i 54-63i 23+8i 65+23i 76+8i 23+23i 90+6i (Only one space)
✘ 2-6i, 4+3i, 7+1i, 34+5i, 7+2i, 23-4i (No comma)
✘ 3-3i,6+2i,10+2i,15+2i,54-3i,1+1i,3-4i
